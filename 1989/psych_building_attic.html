Psych Building Attic
The attic of the (now-demolished) Psychology Building on the Purdue campus was an interesting place.

Accessible via a fire escape, the rough, dusty hardwood floors were strewn with folders, old instruments and 4x5 glass slides of decades-old research results.

The week the building was being demolished, I salvaged an excellent old mirror from one of the bathrooms. It is now installed in my bathroom here in California.
