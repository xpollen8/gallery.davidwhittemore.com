Robert and A Broadripple bridge
Robert Smith (still not that one) and A.

We all hung out quite a bit during this time in my life. She wished to be a model, and she posed often for my camera. Robert was my roommate at the time.

We all took a roadtrip to Broad Ripple, Indiana, which is about as "snooty" as Indianapolis is allowed to get.

The railroad bridge on which they stand was shortly before the genuine article - you could walk for miles, over a couple of rivers. Then the rails were ripped up, and the bridge became a nice, safe walkway.
