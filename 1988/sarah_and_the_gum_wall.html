Sarah and the Gum Wall
Sarah was a sweet girl I knew casually who, knowing I snapped pix, wanted me to do a set for her.

As I shot on slides, I don't think she ever even saw the results; I was unable to print slides easily back then.

So here, Sarah, many years after the fact, is probably the best of the batch from that summer day in West Lafayette. Lovely smile.
