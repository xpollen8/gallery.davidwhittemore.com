Chalky Footprints
I went to see George Clinton and his All Stars in Chicago. Made my own P-Funk t-shirt and everything.

The opening rap group was loud. Too loud. So fucking loud that my hearing shut down that night, only started coming back 2 days later.

To this day, my ears are extremely sensitive to the midrange frequencies they were blasting, and the ringing is a constant companion.
