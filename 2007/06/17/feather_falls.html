Feather Falls
Feather Falls near Oroville, CA. Well worth the 6 mile hike to see. Camping spots 1/4 mile behind the falls are wonderful in the hot of summer.

This is a rough stitched vertical panorama of about 20 frames taken over 10 seconds as I followed the same mass of water down the 600 foot face of the cliff.
