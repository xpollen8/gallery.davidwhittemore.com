gladys, championship pose
This is Gladys. She's my dog, and she's perfect.

We don't know what kind of dog she is, but there's some Lab, some Pit, some hound of some sort. Muscular and playful and FAST - she'll try to get anyone or anything to play the "chasing game" with her. Smart, too.

This is Gladys posing for me on a weekend excursion into Hope Valley in the Sierras, California-side. We went to watch The Leonid Meteor Shower.
