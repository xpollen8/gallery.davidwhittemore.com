Gladys in winter wheat
You betcha... Another "Gladys bounding through a field" snapshot.

These are especially tricky with the Nikon 995 digital camera, as there's about a 1/3 second delay between pressing the button and the picture being taken.

Still, the focus is where it counts, so I'm pleased. Gladys was even more pleased, as she'd been moping inside for a couple of days.
