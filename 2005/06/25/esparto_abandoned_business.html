Esparto Abandoned Business
Across the street from the Esparto Java Cafe (such as it is), at the only major corner in town, is a turn-of-the-previous-century whitewashed red brick building.

Its previous occupant appeared to be a hardware (or maybe feed) store. The interior is currently completely gutted.

Except for a couple of backwards swastikas and a mangey cat.

Handheld, so not as sharp as it could be..
