Nevada Snow Fence
Nevada appears to get all of its precipitation during the winter months.

This is a shot taken illegally from a window opened on the westbound Amtrak California Zephyr train.

On the right-hand side of the photo, you can see distortion caused by the heat coming off of the engine.
